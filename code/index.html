How big can they get?

Jellyfish range from about one millimeter in bell height and diameter to nearly 2 metres in bell height and diameter; the tentacles and mouth parts usually extend beyond this bell dimension.

Where do Jellyfish live?

Jellyfish are found all over the world, from surface waters to the deep sea. Most are exclusively marine, but some live in freshwater.

When did they first appear?

Jellyfish have been in existence for at least 500 million years and possibly 700 million years or more, making them the oldest multi-organ animal group
